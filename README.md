HardWork its time management roguelike. 
The goal of the main game is to earn a set amount of money by working a variety of low-skilled jobs. 
Each job is a minigame, with its own conditions for earning money and penalties for mistakes. 
Completing any job also imposes a certain level of stress on you, which will accumulate until your character dies of a heart attack, ending the game. 
To relieve stress, you need to take a day off from work, one or more days. 
You can't simply stop working, as resting costs money, and at the end of each week you must pay your bills. 
If you fail to pay them, debt collectors will come for you, ending the game.
Finding a balance between working and relaxing while accumulating funds on the way to your cherished goal is the essence of the game.
